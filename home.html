Welcome to Hills Ballet School

We share the joy of dance with children, through a range of fun and dynamic classes that set the foundations of classical ballet technique.

  • Little Tiptoes a creative class for children from 18 months to 3 years to share with mum or dad.
  • Leap into Dance for dancers who are 3 to 5 years old and ready to dance a little more independently.
  • School aged children - Our ballet classes are based on the RAD technique from Pre-Primary onwards. Examinations are available.
